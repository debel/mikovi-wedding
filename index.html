FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

IS IT AN OUTDOOR WEDDING?

The ceremony will take place in one of the Residence Tera gardens. We'll go inside for the dinner and party.

WHAT'S THE DRESS CODE?

We recommend cocktail attire.

For women, this usually means a cocktail dress (but if you're feeling a floor-length gown - go for it!).

For men, cocktail attire is a suit that's not as formal as a black-tie suit would be - or a pair of dress pants and a nice shirt.

Overall, we want you to feel confident in your clothes so there is no strict dress code.

WHAT ARE THE WEDDING COLORS?

We chose gold (#E1C340), teal green (#006D5B), and white.

These colors will be present in many wedding elements, such as decorations and accessories, but we don't expect our guests to follow this color scheme when choosing their clothes.

DO YOU HAVE ANY GIFT RECOMMENDATIONS?

All kinds of gifts are welcome!

From a practical standpoint, we'd prefer to avoid vouchers. 99% of the time when someone gives us a voucher, they expire without us using them.

CAN I BRING FLOWERS?

If you want to bring a bouquet, please feel free to do so! However, we prefer not to receive potted plants. Our cat eats them and there is no way for us to keep them.

HOW CAN I REACH THE LOCATION?

Subway - you should get off the train at the Mladost 1 station. From there you have to walk around 600 meters along Andrei Sakharov Blvd.

Busses 111, 113, 314 or trolley 5. The stop you need is "1400"- Посолствата (The Embassies).

Cab - select the address „Академик Андрей Сахаров“ 56 (Andrei Sakharov 56). You can use the TaxiMe app.

By car.

WHERE CAN WE PARK?

There are a few parking spots at the Residence. You can also park in front of the multifamily building across from the venue.

If you prefer to use the Residence parking (recommended for guests with kids and strollers), please let us know.

WHAT ABOUT THE KIDS?

We'll be happy to see them at our wedding.

We'll provide:

  • kids entertainers;
  • a room next to the dining hall for them to play in;
  • kids menu;
  • coloring books.

We won't have:

  • special high chairs for kids. If you have a small kid that can't sit on an adult chair yet, we recommend bringing a stroller or a high chair for your convenience.
  • their favorite toys. We recommend bringing their favorite book or toy to make them more comfortable.

WAIT... DIDN'T YOU GET MARRIED ALREADY?

We got married on October 10th, 2021 - our third anniversary. Back then we couldn't gather all of our friends and family for a celebration. That's why we'll celebrate together on June 5th, 2022!
